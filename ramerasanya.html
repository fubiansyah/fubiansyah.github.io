Website Development of ramerasanya.com

Client: Nano-nano / Konimex Indonesia

Activity Time : 2014

Nano-nano is a famous Indonesian candy brand with the tagline “rame rasanya”. They absolutely have so many kinds of unique flavor. Can We imagine a candy that has a sweet, sour, and salty mixed flavor?. Yeap so they are giving the “rame rasanya” tag as their brand identity.

Very unique and interesting. it will become Our key point for the next journey of their brand’s identity.

Challenges

early in 2014 most of the brand’s in Indonesia started using Digital marketing strategy. They try to use social media to get engagement, setting the KPI for their social media and also using websites as their tools to communicate with their customers.

PT konimex indonesia came to our team, offering pitch opportunities for their most popular brands “Nano-nano”. Our team gathered to do the brainstorming for the pitching deck. We got the key point of the brand’s.

  • Fun - they don't want to take it seriously
  • Colorful - they want to create the full of color brand's identity
  • Unique - of course their candy have unique flavor

The Social media team have really big idea to create main character called “NANO” is a tenager who never takes anything seriously, cheerful, and always makes jokes around “HIM” . check “nano-nano” social media to get more fun.

As for Us a Developer “nano-nano” website it’s a platform to

  • Introducing the character
  • Sharing their social media activity
  • Sharing the funny story of Nano (article based)

The most important is “creating unusual, unique and interactive websites”.

I was thinking of putting the “nano character” inside the website and making the nano caracter move while the user scrolls the page ~ it's like making the character follow the user when they are scrolling the website. It is impossible if i'm using a vertical scroll so I decided to make the horizontal scroll to make it real.

I am also thinking of putting the character movement (it will look like a cartoon movie on the website), so I need to prepare sprite animation for the character.

the Point of the website component will look like

  • Horizontal Scroll and Parallax overlay section
  • Character sprite animation
  • other jquery plugin
Horizontal Scroll

To make the website scrolling horizontal im using jquery plugin called jInvertScroll

Character sprite animation

I was preparing 8 condition of the character for the sprite animation

  • Start condition with the right direction
  • 2 Moving condition with the right direction
  • Stop move condition (tired look ) with the right direction
  • Start condition with the left direction
  • 2 Moving condition with the the left direction
  • Stop move condition (tired look ) the left direction
