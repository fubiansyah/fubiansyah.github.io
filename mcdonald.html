McDonald Digital Menu Board - Case Study

Client: McDonalds Singapore

Activity Time : July 2016 - Now

In July 2016 I joined one of the digital agencies in Singapore. My role was frontend developer and I was given a challenge to do some greatest work “Creating any Digital Menu Board Content” for one of biggest clients “Mcdonald's Singapore”. Here the short story behind a challenges to creating content for Digital Menu Board

OVERVIEW

The Main purpose of using a digital menu board was to create a different level of customer experience in-store. Digital menu boards are the perfect solution to improve the ordering experience, bringing restaurant menus to life with videos and animations. Content from the digital menu board can be displayed and easily updated, providing value-added information to diners.

While developing the digital menu board we encountered 2 of Customer habits,

  • the customer that has decided what they want to buy
  • and the other one is the one that didn’t know what they want to buy.

Meanwhile they need to up-sell their limited time menu (seasonal menu).

Based on those issue, We got the point from their business objective to create some of "key point" of the Digital menu Board

here the "key point" Objective That We get:

  • How to improve customer experience by making decision easier
  • How to promote high value item
  • How to Increase engagement with impactful visuals

How to improve customer experience by making decision easier

The Digital Menu Board will consist of 2 orientation views (depending on store layout ) Horizontal (landscape view ) and Vertical (portrait view). In early 2016 each store will be installed with a 5 screen Digital menu Board.

To making the customer easy to making decision We try to re-grouping the menu

  • Normal menu (the menu will stay over there and really rare to changes)
  • Limited time offer Menu (seasonal menu)
  • Beverage Menu
  • Happy Meal
Re-grouping Plan of Digital Menu Board

Normal menu will be placed in screen number 1 and number 2, and the screen will be put behind the Cashier order, the reason is to make the customer clearly read the main product information such as menu price.

Limited time offer menu will be placed in screen number 3 (center monitor) and we will give some of animation to made the menu more interactive and become center of attention so user will notice regarding the Seasonal Menu

Beverage menu will be placed on screen number 4. We decided to put the beverage menu after the seasonal menu price, the reason is we will also put some of the McCafe menu on the board so it will be such a teaser for the McCafe drink.

Happy meal menu will be placed on screen number 5 (Last Screen) . The Happy meal is also the signature menu. They want to sell the Happy Meal Toy’s together with the meal. So We made it as a single stand alone content with smiling animation for the happy meal Box.

Digital Menu Board Content technology

The Digital menu board was controlled by the CMS. The cms only allows uploading the content with HTML format. So we are using the latest HTML 5 and CSS 3 technology to develop the content. As We use HTML for content developing We can do so many things, not just putting the video inside the content, but We can also make the content trigger the dynamic weather outside. I was thinking of making the content more dynamic following the weather outside. It's like when it's a rainy day We can only show a hot beverage menu.

Animation from the content can be handled from javascript, we use jquery to handle the animation.

However the content also needs to connect to the CMS API. i can say that the cms system was so powerful. By connecting the content into the CMS API we can do anything like make the price of the menu more dynamic by following the POS pricing.

In Conclusion

Digital menu boards are the perfect solution to improve the ordering experience, bringing restaurant menus to life with videos and animations.

With digital menu boards, restaurant owners can easily update menu items, promotions and prices in real-time by simply revising the data in the content management system.

Digital menu boards present a huge opportunity for upsells and help to boost revenue. Based on orders entered in a POS system, the digital screens can display complementary items. It is an excellent tool for restaurants that offer combos and menu add-ons like sides, drinks, and desserts.

Static menu boards require additional resources to print and install the materials. On the other hand, digital menu boards eliminate the costs of printing and installation, and promote environmental sustainability.
